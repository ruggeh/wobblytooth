About

Rain Station Z is a post-apocalyptic multiplayer survival game set in a world reclaimed by rot, ruin, and the relentless dead. Built on Unreal Engine 5 for PC and Nintendo Switch, it drops players into a hostile open world where resources are scarce, trust is earned, and every decision carries weight.

Atmospheric multiplayer online zombie survival. Follows on from my cult hit Ice Station Z which was originally for Nintendo 3DS. Featuring Missions, Journals and Secret Locations to discover

Key Features

  • Multiplayer SurvivalCo-op, PvP, PvPvE. Become friends by waving to other players. Free text chat with others.
  • Open World ScavengingFood, Ammo, Weapons, Medical supplies, Horse riding, Journals, Jackets, Boots, Backpacks.
  • ZombiesZombie decaying in acid rain. Later dry zombies appear. NPC Survivors can turn into zombies if not stopped.
  • Three Platforms plannedPC via Steam and Nintendo Switch, then PlayStation 5.
  • Acid Rain falls relentlesslyUmbrellas protect your gear from acid rain and are also melee weapons.
